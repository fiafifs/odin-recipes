Quesadilla

This time we're making the best dish ever invented.. QUESADILLAS Let's begin.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz refrigerated cooked chicken breast strips
  • 1/2 cup of salsa
  • 1 package flour tortillas for buriitos
  • Cooking Spray
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup sour cream

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Cut chicken into bite-size pieces. In small bowl, mix chicken and salsa.

  2. Step 2

    3. Spray 1 side of 1 tortilla with cooking spray; place sprayed side down on work surface. Layer with one-fourth of the chicken mixture and 1/2 cup of the cheese. Top with another tortilla; spray top of tortilla with cooking spray.

  3. Step 3

    4. Cook in 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium heat 4 to 6 minutes, carefully turning after 2 minutes, until golden brown. Repeat with remaining tortillas, chicken mixture and cheese. To serve, cut quesadillas into wedges. Serve with sour cream and, if desired, additional salsa.
