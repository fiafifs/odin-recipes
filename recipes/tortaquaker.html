Torta Quaker

And for the grand finale we are making an Argentina original TORTA QUAKER Let's begin.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of butter
  • 2 cups of powder chocolated
  • 100 ml milk
  • 1 bag ladyfingers
  • 2 cups oats

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Melt the butter in a pan and add the cocoa powder and the oats Mix until it becomes one.

  2. Step 2

    3. Wet the ladyfingers in chocolate milk, place them in a serving pan, pour the cocoa butter mix and repeat with as many layter you want and/or can

  3. Step 3

    4. Once every layer is done, place it in the fridge for about 2 to 3 hours and ENJOY!!!
